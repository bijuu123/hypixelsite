Feb 4, 2022 Update: Mandatory Microsoft Account Migration

Hello everyone!

Mojang has just released an announcement regarding the migration of your Minecraft account, you can view this HERE. Starting on March 10th this year, everyone wanting to play the Java edition of Minecraft will be required to migrate their Minecraft account to a Microsoft account. For those who haven't migrated yet, you can find out more information about migrating on our FAQ thread located HERE. We highly recommend going through the migration process earlier than March 10th, both for your overall account security and to avoid not being able to play because you waited until the day of.

We've included some helpful links below as well, so you can stay up to date as we get closer to March 10th.
